# Video Background Instructions

## Required Video File

Place your boxing background video here with the filename: `boxing-background.mp4`

## Video Requirements

- Format: MP4
- Name: boxing-background.mp4
- Content: Boxing-themed footage (training, shadowboxing, etc.)
- Duration: Ideally 20-30 seconds (for smooth looping)
- Quality: Compressed for web (recommend 720p or 1080p depending on file size)
- No audio needed (the video plays muted)

## Attributes Already Configured

The video element is already set up with these attributes:

- autoPlay: Starts automatically
- loop: Repeats continuously
- muted: No sound
- playsInline: Plays inline on mobile devices
- className: "absolute object-cover w-full h-full"

## Where to Find Videos

You can obtain suitable videos from:

1. Stock video sites (Pexels, Shutterstock, etc.)
2. Your own recorded content
3. Licensed footage of boxing/training

Remember to ensure you have the rights to use any video you choose.
